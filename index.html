Breakthrough Maths Website

I led the re-design of the company website to align with a more mature re-branding initiatives. The main goals were to make the website feel less childish and cleaner, while still maintaining personability. I worked with the CEO to make sure all the areas he felt were important to students and parents were accessible on the site, but also to make sure they were arranged in a way to not overwhelm users and display the most important and marketable aspects of the company. I made sure the navigation bar showed the most impartant pages: The different school levels for tutoring, reviews, pricing of the program, and about the school (how this company came to be, how they teach, who their tutors are etc). Then for the footer i would include the other pages that are less frequented, like notes and videos, times the company has been noticed in media outlets, blog posts, etc. Throughout the pages I wanted to create a consistancy in color scheme and feel, so many of the elements are the same style throughout the different pages, and I also would sprinkle relevant reviews on the different pages to consistantly let parents how helpful the srrvice have been for other people. To see the full website, you can visit the site here: https://www.breakthroughmaths.ie/
